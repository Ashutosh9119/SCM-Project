Range Rover

₹ 2.11 Crore  - ₹ 4.38 Crore

The Range Rover is the most luxurious SUV built by Land Rover and is the flagship of their SVO line-up as well. It is offered in both versions – the standard and the long wheelbase with the latter offering more than 100mm of additional knee-room for the rear passenger. The biggest change are new seats which are now wider with multiple control functions available at the touch of a button. Obviously the cabin is wrapped in the choicest of leather and gets the premium wooden veneers for the luxurious feel.

You get a variety of engine options like a 3.0-litre V6 petrol, a 4.4-litre diesel V8 and the 5.0-litre supercharged V8. All models get a ZF eight-speed AT and the latest version of Land Rover’s terrain response system.
